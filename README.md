Classes.bg is a digital platform built for schools specializing in dance, sports, arts, and languages. It helps school owners move away from manual paperwork and disorganized chat groups by providing a professional infrastructure for their daily operations.

## What it does

The platform serves as a central hub for school management. It simplifies the connection between administrators, instructors, and parents. Instead of tracking attendance in notebooks or chasing payments in Viber, everything happens in one digital ecosystem.

## Core Features

* **Hierarchical Access:** Dedicated tools for administrators, teaching staff, and parents or students.
* **Centralized Profiles:** Users can manage multiple school enrollments and different groups through a single account.
* **Smart Scheduling:** An interactive calendar that tracks events and lets teachers mark attendance or absences in real time.
* **Payment Integration:** Secure online payments through Stripe for monthly fees, special events, and equipment.
* **Automated Communication:** Direct notifications sent via Viber and email to keep parents informed without the noise of massive group chats.
* **Accessibility:** The platform works across all devices through a responsive web interface and a dedicated mobile application.
* **Multilingual Support:** Full support for both Bulgarian and English users.
